Welcome to Sudoku Kolor

Sudoku Kolor is much more than a game. It's where you put your Memory, Patience, Perseverance, and Logic into action.

The striking thing about Sudoku Kolor isn't just the different colored numbers, which makes searching easier. Unlike normal Sudokus, Sudoku Kolor relies on a unique solution. This means there is only one valid solution: the original template with all 81 numbers in their correct spots. Standard Sudokus often allow various number combinations as long as they follow the basic rule of not repeating numbers in rows or columns.

To help you complete the board, you start with 3 hints. After using them, you can try combinations in the empty cells until you find the original numbers. That's when the "Row", "Column", or "Block Completed" notifications become crucial‚Äîthey verify that those numbers are placed correctly.

You strictly must follow the standard rule: do not repeat a number more than once in each row or column, otherwise, it will be marked as an error.

Tip: Use your memory to remember the combinations you try. Sometimes it's about remembering where you placed 3 or 4 numbers, and that's when the game gets interesting. You can only make 5 errors per level (10 levels total).

You have all the time you need to reach Level 10. I just recommend not playing too late at night, because the game will grab you and steal your sleep hours. I say this from experience. Enjoy it!
